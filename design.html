Welcome to ENZO

Who are we?

The mission of Enzo is to ensure our drivers feel like winners no matter the destination they are headed towards. They are the winners of their lives and are in full control. This is because “Enzo” means the winner in Italian. The battery on the bottom of the car: For larger cars/SUVs, the center of gravity is much higher which causes it to rollover much easier. Since the batteries are on the bottom, the center of gravity is lower which allows for much lower chances of rollovers. This ensures the safety of our electric cars.
